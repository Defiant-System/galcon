Basic Gameplay

Click to select your planet.
Indiciated by rotating blue dashed line.

Then Choose a target. Click on or
drag to the enemy planet.

Production

The number on the planet is its fleet size.
Large planets produce more ships.
Gray planet are neutral.

Take over the neutral planets so that you can
produce enough ships to conquer the enemy.

Advanced Controls

Select multiple planets by clicking
them individually, or select all by double
clicking one of them.

Change attack fleet size in the toolbar.

Conquer the enemy to win!
